HEADERS (two styles)
==

Setext-style
--

| HEAD LEVEL            | SYNTAX                        |
|-----------------------|-------------------------------|
| A first level header  | underline with equal signs(=) |
| A second level header | underline with hyphens(-)     |

## atx-style
| HEAD LEVEL | SYNTAX                                             |
|------------|----------------------------------------------------|
| 1-6 level  | put 1-6 hash marks(#) at the beginning of the line |
